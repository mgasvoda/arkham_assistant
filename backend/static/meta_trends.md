# Meta Trends and Strategies

This file contains current meta analysis and popular strategies.

## Popular Archetypes

### Seeker: Investigation Focus
- High intellect, clue-gathering specialists
- Card draw and consistency
- Action compression

### Guardian: Combat Focus
- High combat, enemy management
- Damage dealing and tanking
- Asset-heavy builds

### Rogue: Economy & Flexibility
- Resource generation
- Evasion and mobility
- Skill cards and flex slots

### Mystic: Spell-based
- Willpower tests, spells
- Support and utility
- Encounter deck manipulation

### Survivor: Recursion & Resilience
- Playing from discard pile
- Low-cost efficiency
- Chaos token manipulation
